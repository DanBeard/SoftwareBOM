Bill of Materials (BOM)

A list of the raw materials, sub-assemblies, intermediate assemblies, sub-components, parts and the quantities of each needed to manufacture an end product

Software (In the age of IoT)

A magic box running a 10 year old OS, using out-of-date 3rd party libraries, hard coded credentials and an internet connection that can affect the real world
